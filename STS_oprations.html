STS Transfers. Precision. Safety. Compliance.

Evita Ship Management specializes in highly efficient, safe, and fully compliant Ship-to-Ship transfer operations for liquid and dry bulk cargoes. Our meticulous planning and execution minimize downtime and maximize your operational effectiveness across key maritime hubs worldwide:

Strict Risk Mitigation: Comprehensive pre-operation calculations including dynamic mooring analyses, fender layout design, and continuous local weather monitoring.

POAC Supervision: Every operation is overseen by highly qualified Person in Overall Advisory Control (POAC) experts strictly observing OCIMF and ICS/SIGTTO guidelines.

Specialized Vapor Balancing: For volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and gas carriers, specialized equipment is deployed to completely mitigate chemical venting or atmospheric loss.
