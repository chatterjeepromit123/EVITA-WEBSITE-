Expertise in Sale and Purchase

Acquiring or disposing of a vessel is one of the most significant decisions a shipowner will make. Evita's S&P advisory services provide comprehensive pre-purchase inspections and in-depth technical assessments that give buyers complete confidence in the condition and value of any prospective acquisition.

We guide owners and buyers through every stage of the transaction — from initial due diligence and survey management to documentation, delivery, and post-purchase technical onboarding — ensuring a smooth and fully informed process.
