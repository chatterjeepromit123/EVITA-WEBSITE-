About Evita

Who We Are

Built for the World's Most Complex Vessels

Headquartered in Mumbai, Evita Ship Management has rapidly established itself as a top-tier partner for vessel owners and operators handling high-consequence liquid and gas cargos. We don't manage ordinary ships — we manage floating, high-pressure thermodynamic process plants.

In the dynamic and highly demanding world of maritime trade, Evita Ship Management Pvt. Ltd. has rapidly emerged as a beacon of reliability, safety, and operational excellence. We offer a comprehensive suite of ship management and specialized maritime services, positioning ourselves as a pivotal partner for the global energy and chemical supply chains. Our unwavering commitment to quality and strict vetting standards has established us as a top-tier service provider for vessel owners and operators worldwide.

What sets Evita Ship Management apart is our deep, specialized focus on complex asset classes, including Gas Carriers, Oil Tankers, and Chemical Tankers. Managing these sophisticated vessels requires an uncompromising approach to safety and compliance. Our team of seasoned marine professionals brings a wealth of technical expertise to the table, seamlessly navigating stringent international regulations, environmental mandates, and fluctuating market dynamics with absolute precision.

As a true "one-stop solution" for specialized tonnage, we are committed to maximizing asset value and operational efficiency. By leveraging data-driven insights, Evita Ship Management optimizes routes, enhances fuel efficiency, and aggressively mitigates risks—ensuring that high-consequence cargo is delivered safely, sustainably, and on time, every time.
