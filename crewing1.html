Crew Management

Right People. The Right Positions. Every Time.
We believe human resources are the most vital asset in shipping. Beyond technical knowledge, attitude defines success. Our crew management model is built on precision placement — matching exceptional seafarers to roles where they excel, delivering a true one-stop solution for ship owners.

Operating out of Mumbai—the premier hub for elite maritime talent—Evita curates a highly vetted pool of officers and crew specialized in dangerous liquid and gas cargos. Current operations utilize strict pre-joining vetting protocols and continuous evaluation matrices. Through targeted maritime training programs, seafarers undergo intensive simulation-based training, cargo-specific hazard management briefings, and rigorous safety-culture familiarization before deployment.

Building Competent Teams

The quality of a vessel's crew is the single most important factor in safe, efficient operations. Evita conducts rigorous recruitment and screening processes, deploying only qualified officers and ratings who meet or exceed STCW requirements and our own stringent internal standards.
Beyond recruitment, we invest in structured training programmes focused on safety culture, emergency preparedness, and continuous professional development — ensuring your crew is always ready for the challenges of modern seafaring.
